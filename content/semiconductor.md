---
title: Semiconductor
---
A semiconductor is a material that can act as both a conductor and an insulator depending on conditions.

It is the foundation of modern electronics because it can control the flow of electric current.

# Common Materials
* Silicon
    * The most commonly used semiconductor material.
* Germanium
    * Used in some specialized electronic devices.

# Key Idea

Semiconductors are useful because their electrical behavior can be controlled.

This makes them important for building electronic components such as:

* diodes
* transistors
* integrated circuits
* computer chips
* sensors
* memory devices

# Doping

Doping means adding small amounts of impurities to a semiconductor to change its electrical properties.

There are two main types:

* N-type semiconductor
    * Has extra electrons.
* P-type semiconductor
    * Has extra holes.

# Simple Example

A transistor uses semiconductor material to control current flow.
This allows computers and phones to process information.
