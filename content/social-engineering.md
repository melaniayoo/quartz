---
title: Social Engineering
---
Social Engineering is a cyberattack method that tricks people instead of attacking systems directly.

Attackers use human emotions like trust, fear, urgency, or curiosity to make people reveal sensitive information or take unsafe actions.

# Common Examples
* Phishing
    * Fake emails or messages that try to steal passwords or personal information.
* Impersonation
    * Pretending to be someone trusted, like a coworker, manager, or IT support.
* Pretexting
    * Creating a fake story to convince someone to share information.
