title: Risk Management
---

Risk Management is the process of identifying, assessing, and reducing risks that could harm an organization.

In cybersecurity, risk usually means the chance that a threat will exploit a vulnerability and cause damage.

# Main Steps
1. Identify Risks
* Find possible threats, vulnerabilities, and assets that need protection.
2. Assess Risks
* Understand how likely the risk is and how serious the impact would be.
3. Prioritize Risks
* Focus on the risks that could cause the most damage.
4. Treat Risks
* Decide how to handle the risk.

# Risk Treatment Options
* Avoid
    * Stop doing the risky activity.
* Reduce
    * Add security controls to lower the risk.
* Transfer
    * Share the risk with another party, such as insurance.
* Accept
    * Accept the risk if it is low or unavoidable.

# Simple Example

If a company stores customer data, there is a risk of data leakage.
The company can reduce this risk by using encryption, access control, and regular security monitoring.
